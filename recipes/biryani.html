Bombay Biryani

Description

This traditionally prepared biyani has taken over the internet by storm.

Basmati rice mixed in with marinated chicken, comprosing of 12 unique flavours that release enough dopamine as would a pack of cigerettes.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Bombay biryani seasoning
  • Whole chicken

Steps To Prepare

  1. Rinse rice and chicken thoroughly
  2. Marinate the chicken
  3. Dazzle the rice with bombay biryani powder - 2 tbsn
  4. Place in a cooking pot for 2 hours
  5. Give it a mix with wooden spoon & serve hot
